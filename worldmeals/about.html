About WorldMeals

Travel the world without leaving your kitchen.

WorldMeals is a global cooking platform built to help people discover dishes, plan affordable meals, use what they already have, and cook with confidence.

🌍 Our mission

Make global food discovery simple, useful, and welcoming for everyone. We believe every recipe carries a story and every kitchen can become a doorway to another culture.

🚀 Our vision

Build the world’s most useful cooking companion by combining global dishes, practical planning tools, intelligent guidance, and an experience that feels effortless.

What WorldMeals helps you do

  • 🌎 Explore dishes by country and region
  • 🤖 Get cooking help from Chef AI
  • 📷 Scan fridge ingredients for ideas
  • 📅 Plan meals across the week
  • 💰 Stay within a food budget
  • 🛒 Build a smart grocery list
  • ❤️ Save favorite dishes
  • 🎛️ Filter by diet, calories, protein, spice, time, and cost

❤️ Our promise

Every WorldMeals feature should make cooking easier, clearer, or more enjoyable. We value usefulness over clutter, guidance over confusion, and respect for the cultures behind the food we share.
